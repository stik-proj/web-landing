Whitepaper

Vision

We connect the points that shine independently.

P2E, NFT, and Security Token are like the milk and honey of the upcoming metaverse. However,
in the area of investment, each digital asset operates individually,
loses synergy, and is not prepared for a crisis. Staika decided to strengthen these points
by connecting them to a line. We have formed a more stable and efficient investment
environment by connecting the strengths of the decentralized blockchain to Staika's services.

Problem

Stika focuses
on these problems.

memory of
inconvenience

Existing cryptocurrency electronic wallets are difficult for the general public to understand and use due to UX based on the decentralized method.

Unstable investment

In an open investment platform that anyone can participate in, risks have arisen due to various split owners.

lack of gamability

The simple design and low gameability of the existing P2E games have increased user fatigue.

Plunge in value

The value of artwork sold on the basis of simple popularity without a recognized valuation soon plummeted.

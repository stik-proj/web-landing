Whitepaper

Vision

We connect the points that shine independently.

Digital asset, M2E service, and the tokenization of real assets are like the milk and honey of the upcoming metaverse. However,
in the area of investment, each digital asset operates Individually,
loses synergy, and is not prepared for a crisis. Staika decided to strengthen these points
by connecting them to a line. We have formed a more stable and efficient investment
environment by connecting the strengths of the decentralized blockchain to Staika's services.

Problem

Stika focuses
on these problems.

memory of
inconvenience

Existing cryptocurrency electronic wallets are difficult for the general public to understand and use due to UX based on the decentralized method.

Unstable investment

In an open investment platform that anyone can participate in, risks have arisen due to various split owners.

Initial barriers to entry

Existing M2E services can be started by purchasing expensive NFT equipment with unstable value.

Plunge in value

The value of digital assets sold on the basis of simple popularity without a recognized valuation soon plummeted.

Ecosystem

We gather the lines together
to create a flexible and stable face.

Staika's services, which connect each independent value,
come together to create a platform for customers to grow to their heart's content.

    digital wallet

  • Experiential M2E service

  • Trekking to Earn service

  • Convenient NFT trading platform with user-friendly UI-UX

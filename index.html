Vision

Each of them connects the shining dots together.

The tokenization of digital assets, M2E services, P2E games, and real assets is a step forward for the
next generation. However, each digital asset failed to create synergy in the area of investment and was
unable to weather the crisis. Staika decided to consolidate the shiny dots that had been separated like
this into one. By connecting the strengths of the decentralized blockchain to the Staika project,
we have created a more stable and efficient environment.

Problem

Stika focuses
on these problems.

memory of
inconvenience

Existing cryptocurrency electronic wallets are difficult for the general public to understand and use due to UX based on the decentralized method.

Unstable investment

In an open investment platform that anyone can participate in, risks have arisen due to various split owners.

Initial barriers to entry

Existing M2E services can be started by purchasing expensive NFT equipment with unstable value.

The forgotten essence

Mass-produced P2E games focused on token value rather than game content appeared, having a prejudice that P2E games are less entertaining.
